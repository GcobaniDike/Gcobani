    How to connect with us?

    Step 1: Connect with us


    Visit our FUNDA HELPING PROGRAM page and provide us with your area and subject requirements. We'll be in touch within the hour with our tutoring packages and rates. Identify preferred days and times for lessons and any special learning requirements. We'll connect you with one of our amazing tutors!

    Step 2:Tutor Matched


    An experienced consultant will select one of our pre-screened tutors, based on your needs and preferences. Approve your tutor's profile and purchase a lesson package that best suits your needs and budget. Everything has been finalised and you eagerly await your first lesson!

    Step 3: Start learning

    At the first lesson, a Learner Needs Analysis will be conducted to establish problem areas and set learning goals. Tutoring will then begin immediately! We will be in touch every step of the way to ensure the best possible tutoring experience!
